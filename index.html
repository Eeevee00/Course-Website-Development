About Us

Eve Academy was established during pandemic era in year 2020, where online learning becomes a norm.

Every student deserves a chance to pursue the education they envisioned for, and this is why we are here to provide.

We're a small academy made up of a group of passionate lecturers that values every student's learning experience and honing their practical skills that is applicable in the IT world.

Our programmes are suited for virtual learning, with self-paced tutorials, and certified course certificates.

We conduct classes through interactive sessions and live demonstrations to keep up with the standards and cater to the online-pace learning.

Do explore more on us to view upon the programmes that we're offering.

Thank you and hope to see you being part of us !
